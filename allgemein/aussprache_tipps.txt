PRONUNICATION
==========
1. Ich, mich, dich, sich, milch - pronunce as "sh" in English like sh in push,cash etc
2. Straße, heißen - pronunce like "ss" in English like miss,kiss etc
3. der Käse, das Mädchen - pronunce like "a" in English like aeroplane,angel
4. der Löffel, schön 
5. die Tür, über
6. kn, Knoblauch
7. e at the end of the word Lampe, Katze pronunce as 'a' in english 
8. Ps, Psychiater psychiatrist pronounce both letters
9. Pf, pfeffer, pflanze, Pflanzen
10. a, i, o, u , e
11. AU — “Ow” (Like in “how.”) Schauen 
12. ÄU — “Oy” (Like in “boy”) die Bäume 
13. EI — “Ai” (Like the letter “I” in English) Steigen, eigen
14. EU — “Oy” Europa, Euro, beutel
15. IE — “Ee” (Like “E” in English) Fliegen
16  CH - Kh ( Loch, Lauch )
17. CK — “K” (Like the “K” in “sick.”) Stück
18. PH — “F” (Like the “ph” sound in “philosophy.”) Phui
19. SCH — “Sh” (Like in “shoot.”) Deutsch
20. SP — “Shp” (it’s like the “sp” in Spiel, Sport, Sprechen)
21. ST — “Sht” like Stück
